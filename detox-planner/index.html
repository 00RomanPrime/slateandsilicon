DIAG-01 // Pre-Experiment Diagnostic

5 Minutes of Silence

Set a timer for 5 minutes. No phone. No screen. No audio. Sit. Notice what pulls on you. Notice what your mind reaches for. You are not trying to relax. You are reading the signal.

Duration: 5 min // Observation only
DIAG-02 // Pre-Experiment Diagnostic

10 Minutes, No Phone

Leave your phone in another room. Not silenced — removed. Wait 10 minutes. Track the impulse to check it. Most people don't avoid this. They avoid what it reveals.

Duration: 10 min // Observation only

If either diagnostic produced discomfort → you are in the right place. Proceed to EXP-01.
