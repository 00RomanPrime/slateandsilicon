Why This Exists

I thought I had a discipline problem.

I got off work early one afternoon. I had plans. Real ones — watch something, play some games, decompress. A few hours became an all-nighter. By the time the sun came through the window, a full workday was an hour and a half away.

I hadn't rested. I hadn't been entertained. I had just been online. Aimlessly. Without ever deciding to be.

When I finally got quiet enough to ask honest questions, I found the answer: this wasn't a character flaw. This was engineering.
