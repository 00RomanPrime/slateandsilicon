DIAG-01 // Pre-Experiment Diagnostic

5 Minutes of Silence

Set a timer for 5 minutes. No phone. No screen. No audio. Sit with it. Notice what your mind reaches for, and when. You're not trying to relax. You're reading the baseline.

Duration: 5 min // Observation only
DIAG-02 // Pre-Experiment Diagnostic

10 Minutes, No Phone

Leave your phone in another room. Not silenced. Removed. Wait 10 minutes. Notice when the impulse to check it arrives. Notice what triggered it. That's the data.

Duration: 10 min // Observation only
